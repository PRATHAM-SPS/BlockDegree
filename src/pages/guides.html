PLEASE READ THESE HOW TO USE BLOCKDEGREE PLATFORM

1. How to register?

You can click on the login button and login via either your social accounts on Google, Linkedin, Twitter & Facebook or can register via Email.

2. How to start a course?

There is Online Blockchain Training on the navigation bar, once you hover over it, three courses will open in the dropdown:-
  • Blockchain Basic course for an engineer
  • Blockchain Advanced course for an engineer.
  • Blockchain professional course for an engineer.
  • Cloud Computing course for an engineer.
    • Click on any given course, then you can Click on Start The Free Course. After completing the study curriculum, you can take the exam by clicking on the TAKE EXAM button on the navigation bar.

    3. How to give an exam?

    Click on TAKE EXAM , then ENROLL. Once you’ve enrolled, you get an option to take a particular course in which you have enrolled on the same page.

    4. Where you can see your Certificate?

    Click on “Get My Certificates” you see your certificate hash and document hash.

  • Copy of certificate hash and paste on the area "Enter the hash" and click on “Open certificate” button. On clicking “Open Certificate” a pop-up will be opened displaying your certificate
  • Copy of document hash and paste on the area "Enter the hash" and click on “Open certificate” button. On clicking “Open Certificate” a pop-up will be opened displaying your document that contains Marks and Percentage.

    • Note: We have provided you with the FAQ if you have more questions please Contact us.
